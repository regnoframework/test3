FAQ

Documentation
  • I'd like to make a change or addition to the Regno Standard, how can I do this?
    The Regno Standard is governed and maintained by a non profit and non commercial Regno Standard Working Group. All changes and updates of the Regno Standard are handled by the Working Group.

    The Working Group are keen to develop the Regno Standard so that is satisfies a diverse range of data requirements; they are interested in any changes, additions or comments that you have regarding the standard. The group may be contacted directly here.

  • Is it possible to get a PDF copy of the Regno Standard?
    Yes, a full free PDF version of the Regno Standard is available directly from our commercial partner Regno Software. You can request a full version of the PDF by contacting them directly here.
Licensing and Ownership
  • How is the Regno Standard licensed?
    The Regno Standard is an Open Standard, licensed under the permissive free software MIT License, designed to be used without fees, royalties or restrictions by any individual, group or organisation.

    For more details including the full license please visit the Licence Section.

  • Who owns and maintains the Regno Standard?
    The Regno Standard is owned, developed and maintained by the non profit and non commercial Regno Standard Working Group.

    If you would like more details about the Regno Standard Working Group or would like to become involved, please contact us here.
Security
  • How is security implemented in the Regno Standard?
    The Regno Standard includes scope for Identity documents that are used to set granular document ownership and access rights. API implementations use this functionality for high level rights control, additional security is handled at the network storage and Cloud level.

Using the Regno Standard
  • Who uses the Regno Standard?
    The Regno Standard is currently used by a diverse range of commerical and private organisations including Formula 1 and Touring Car teams, event timing system providers, financial trading institutions and by pharmaceutical companies.

    If you are using the Regno Standard we'd love to hear about it, drop us an email here.
